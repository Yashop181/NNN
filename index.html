About Us

Founded in 2022 in the suburbs of New York, the NNN is an Indo-American fusion dance collective for youth-led initiative celebrating diversity through movement. What began as a small group of students inspired by childhood exposure to folk traditions has grown into a vibrant community of performers.

We weave Indian classical and regional styles with American hip-hop, jazz, and contemporary dance‚Äî creating performances that speak in many languages, both cultural and artistic.
